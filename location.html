Location

Ikshaa is located in Loutulim, South Goa which is among Goa's prettiest villages with narrow winding roads and impressive heritage mansions

  • 20 minute drive from Goa airport
  • 10 minute drive from Margao railway station
  • 15-minute drive from the virgin beaches of South Goa
