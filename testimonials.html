  • Sonny Walia, New Delhi, India

    I reached Ikshaa with very little expectations as I have experienced in the past that what is normally seen over the internet is very different to the reality. But this beautiful villa was more than what I had hoped for.

    Ikshaa is one of the most secluded, romantic and luxurious villa I have been to. What is most appealing to it is the fact that it combines luxury with rustic beauty. From the road one can’t even imagine that there lies a 3 bedroom structure. The greenery and forest around was charming. I have to give 10/10 to the landlady, Carmen who designed and built this structure with so much care and love. Small things which eventually make a big difference like sanitation, water supply, water pressures, lighting, etc were perfect. Everything was well planned. The bedrooms, the bathrooms and the sitting area, all fantastic.

    The only company me and my wife had was the monkeys in the distance and peacocks in our yard (I will ignore the occasional frog in the courtyard). Peaceful and quiet. The private swimming pool within the villa is ideal for a small family.

    The villa is super but the structure makes for only a fraction of the story. The luxury and beauty of this place is complimented by the warmth and smile of the friendly landlady, Carmen. She welcomed us and was there throughout, only a call away to take care of all our needs. The housekeeper Anthony deserves a special mention as he with his ever eager and pleasing nature took very good care of us.

    Now a very important part. The FOOD. It was a gastronomical treat. The best local Goan food I have ever had. Every meal was different and every meal was awesome. Having Carmen's home cooked food in generous helpings is as important as anything else to make a perfect holiday.

    I STRONGLY RECOMMEND IKSHAA (It’s a AAA place) to anyone who wants a beautiful, quiet, secluded, ROMANTIC and yet LUXURIOUS holiday. And I compliment Carmen on doing such a splendid job.

    I have found my place in Ikshaa and I hope to return on regular basis.
