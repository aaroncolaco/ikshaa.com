About

A hidalgo's villa? A nineteenth century Goan mansion? A Hindu temple? A resort? Ikshaa in pristine Loutolim is a harmonious blend of all of these and is the ultimate heritage luxury villa accommodation in Goa for people seeking refuge from big-city stress. It has the comforts of modern living in South Goa but all the earthy charm of the Goa of yesteryears down to the little recesses in the walls for the old ponttios or paraffin lamps!

Ikshaa is the best Goa holiday experience because this villa, in the heart of a laid back village of Loutolim in South Goa is a magnificent dwelling, reminiscent of the quintessential Goa way back in the sixties. Yet it is modern and luxurious, with 5-star rooms and amenities including a wifi network that covers the entire villa, the pool area and the lawns.

Why Ikshaa?

..........because that's the way Goa was always meant to be!
