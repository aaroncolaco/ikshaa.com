About

A hidalgo's villa? A nineteenth century Goan mansion? A Hindu temple? A resort?
Ikshaa in pristine Loutolim is a harmonious blend of all of these and is the ultimate heritage luxury villa accommodation in Goa for people seeking refuge from big-city stress. It has the comforts of modern living in South Goa but all the earthy charm of the Goa of yesteryears down to the little recesses in the walls for the old ponttios or paraffin lamps!

Don't just imagine Goa. Live it, at Ikshaa!
